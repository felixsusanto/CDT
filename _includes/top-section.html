Moving forward with the company’s direction to transform experience in customer care. It requires us to extend our design knowledge to learn new skill of UX fundamentals and Front End in building better design solutions. Our goal is to deliver design that is not only visually pleasing but also meet the user needs.

With the new direction, we feel there is a need to make our team’s name more intuitive and self-explanatory that portrays the team expertise. Looking at standard practise in design industry, we decided to make a change to call ourselves “Creative Design Team”. A team that responsible to develop conceptual ideas that convey message by creating aesthetic visuals to deliver mockup, animation, visual design, motion design, design research and usability. Act as a design evangelist, we coordinate design principles and practices inside WDS Company.
